Fractional Service Tiers

With Steel Muse, you engage innovation roles on a fractional basis. Each role provides a distinct tier of service — delivering world-class results. Pricing is tailored to your specific needs and the scope of services. And as your venture grows, Steel Muse can scale with you.

Director of Research
Works with inventors and technologists to map out the competitive and technical landscape surrounding their ideas — identifying existing patents, market gaps, and scientific advancements to define clear targets and boundaries for new inventions, intellectual property, and venture opportunities.
Director of Communications
Works with client teams to craft compelling narratives and messaging around new ideas and innovations — translating complex technical or scientific knowledge into engaging explanations, presentations, and thought leadership content that resonates with investors, partners, and the market.
Innovation Analyst
Works closely with patent agents and legal teams to support the patent application process — meticulously analyzing patent claims, examiner objections, and existing "prior art" to prepare robust arguments and secure valuable patents.
Innovation Manager
Collaborates with patent attorneys, R&D teams, and inventors to strategically oversee new invention specifications and manage entire patent portfolios — ensuring that intellectual property is aligned with business goals and developed into truly valuable, defensible assets.
VP Strategic Innovation
Works directly with investors, founders, and executives to define the high-level strategic direction for inventions, intellectual property, and new ventures — aligning innovation efforts with overall business objectives and market opportunities, turning strategy into actionable execution.
Chief Innovation Officer
Orchestrates the entire innovation lifecycle at the executive level — providing comprehensive innovation leadership, from identifying emerging opportunities to building robust internal systems for continuous invention, intellectual property management, and successful venture creation.
